Erectile Dysfunction

It is important to achieve and maintain an erection firm enough to carry out sexual intercourse and satisfy their partner. Due to physical or psychological reasons, erection can be difficult but not always then this condition is called erectile dysfunction.

Causes Of Erectile Dysfunction

The problem of Erectile Dysfunction occurs due to

  • Bad Habits

    Smoking, consumption of liquor, drugs & tobacco can affect the ability to achieve an erection.

  • Surgeries

    Surgeries of the reproductive organs can impact the ability to achieve & maintain an erection.

  • Psychological Issues

    Stress, depression and anxiety can trouble the signals between the brain and penis to erect.

  • Health conditions

    Health problems like blood pressure, diabetes and obesity can affect the ability to erect.

  • Relationship Issues

    Unsolved conflicts between couples before sex can affect the ability to achieve an erection.

  • Medications

    Some anti-depressants & hormone medications are packed with warnings of erectile dysfunction.
